About me


I am Martin Marin, born in Colombia with an Argentinian heart.

Argentina was the country that opened the doors for me in the world of tattoos, I chose this career 7 years ago. I started 8 years ago but it took me a year to be fully in.

My first job in a tattoo studio was 2 years after I started. Tattoos are a journey without a learning end line, to this day I am still learning new techniques and with each design, I try to add more. I want each individual to have something unique done by me.

Right now, I believe I excel in all tattooing styles and I like them all. Every idea a customer brings to my attention, I develop it and  I adapt it to their desires. I look for something unique and different, delicate and harmonic to their body.

When people see my tattoos, I want them to recognise them, to know that they are mine. I always said that tattoos are a moving art, they are my way to show and put something out there in the world, forever.

Today I am based in Spain, looking for new challenges, new ideas, new techniques to offer more to every single one of you.

I invite you to follow my work, to comment and share, I look to show my knowledge by leaving something unique in people’s bodies.
